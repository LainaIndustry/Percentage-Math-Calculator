Privacy Policy

Last Updated: 2024

Introduction

Welcome to PercentageMath ("we," "our," or "us"). We operate the PercentageMath website and related percentage calculator tools (the "Service").

This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, disclose, and safeguard your information when you use our Service. We respect your privacy and are committed to protecting it through our compliance with this policy.

By using the Service, you agree to the collection and use of information in accordance with this Privacy Policy.

Information We Collect

Personal Information

We do not require registration to use our calculator tools. We collect minimal personal information only when you voluntarily provide it:

  • Contact Information: If you contact us via email or contact form, we may collect your name, email address, and message content.
  • Communication Data: Correspondence when you contact us for support or inquiries.

Non-Personal Information

We automatically collect certain information when you visit our Service:

  • Usage Data: Information about how you interact with our Service, including pages visited, time spent, and features used.
  • Technical Data: Browser type, device type, operating system, IP address, and approximate location (country-level).
  • Cookies Data: See our Cookie Policy section below for details.

Calculator Data

Important: All percentage calculations happen locally in your browser. We do not collect, store, or transmit your calculation data. Your calculations remain private on your device.

How We Use Your Information

We use the collected information for various purposes:

  • To provide and maintain our Service
  • To improve, personalize, and expand our Service
  • To understand and analyze how you use our Service
  • To develop new features and functionality
  • To communicate with you, either directly or through one of our partners
  • To send you updates, support messages, and administrative information
  • To prevent fraud and enhance security
  • To comply with legal obligations

Cookies and Tracking Technologies

We use cookies and similar tracking technologies to track activity on our Service and hold certain information.

Types of Cookies We Use

  • Essential Cookies: Necessary for the Service to function properly.
  • Preference Cookies: Remember your settings and preferences.
  • Analytics Cookies: Help us understand how visitors interact with our Service.
  • Advertising Cookies: Used to deliver relevant advertisements (see Advertising section).

Your Cookie Choices

You can instruct your browser to refuse all cookies or to indicate when a cookie is being sent. However, if you do not accept cookies, some parts of our Service may not function properly.

Advertising and Third-Party Services

Google AdSense

We use Google AdSense to display advertisements on our Service. Google AdSense is a web advertising service provided by Google LLC.

Google AdSense uses "cookies" to serve ads based on your visit to our Service and other sites on the Internet. You may opt out of personalized advertising by visiting Google Ads Settings.

For more information about Google AdSense, please visit Google's Advertising Privacy & Terms.

Third-Party Services

We may employ third-party companies and individuals to facilitate our Service ("Service Providers"), to provide the Service on our behalf, or to assist us in analyzing how our Service is used.

These third parties have access to your information only to perform these tasks on our behalf and are obligated not to disclose or use it for any other purpose.

Data Security

The security of your data is important to us. We implement appropriate technical and organizational measures to protect your personal information.

However, no method of transmission over the Internet or electronic storage is 100% secure. While we strive to use commercially acceptable means to protect your personal information, we cannot guarantee its absolute security.

Local Processing Advantage

Our percentage calculators process all data locally in your browser. This means:

  • Your calculations never leave your device
  • No calculation data is transmitted to our servers
  • Enhanced privacy for sensitive calculations
  • Faster performance with no network latency

Children's Privacy

Our Service is suitable for all ages, including children. However, we do not knowingly collect personally identifiable information from children under 13.

If you are a parent or guardian and you are aware that your child has provided us with personal information, please contact us. If we become aware that we have collected personal information from children without verification of parental consent, we take steps to remove that information from our servers.

Data Retention

We retain personal information only for as long as necessary to fulfill the purposes outlined in this Privacy Policy, unless a longer retention period is required or permitted by law.

When we no longer need to process your personal information, we will either delete or anonymize it.

Your Data Protection Rights

Depending on your location, you may have certain rights regarding your personal information:

  • Access: Request copies of your personal information.
  • Rectification: Request correction of inaccurate information.
  • Erasure: Request deletion of your personal information.
  • Restriction: Request restriction of processing your personal information.
  • Objection: Object to our processing of your personal information.
  • Portability: Request transfer of your data to another organization.
  • Withdraw Consent: Withdraw your consent at any time.

To exercise these rights, please contact us using the information in the Contact Us section.

Changes to This Privacy Policy

We may update our Privacy Policy from time to time. We will notify you of any changes by posting the new Privacy Policy on this page and updating the "Last Updated" date.

You are advised to review this Privacy Policy periodically for any changes. Changes to this Privacy Policy are effective when they are posted on this page.
